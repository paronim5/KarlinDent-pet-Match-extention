# Policlinic Management – Administrator Manual

## Login

- Open the web application in your browser.
- Sign in with your administrator email and password.

## Navigation

The left navigation contains:

- Clinic – overview and P&L
- Income – patient payments
- Outcome – expenses and salaries
- Staff – staff directory and management

## Clinic page

- See monthly lease cost, average payment per patient, and average salaries by role.
- Explore the daily P&L chart for the selected period.
- Use “Export CSV” or “Export PDF” to download the P&L report for external analysis.

## Income page

- Search for an existing patient, or leave the selection empty and fill in the “New patient” fields to create a new patient.
- Select the treating doctor.
- Enter payment amount, method (cash or card), and an optional note.
- Save the payment to record it as clinic income.
- Use the date filters under “Income records” to review daily or monthly income and see each visit.

## Outcome page

- Use “Record expense” to register material, utility, equipment, or other expenses.
- Select a category, enter amount, date, vendor, and description, then save.
- Use “Record salary payment” to pay doctors, assistants, administrators, or janitors. Select staff member, amount, payment date, and note, then save.
- The tables show all expenses and salary payments for the selected period.
- The monthly outcome summary chart shows expenses vs salaries per month; use “Export CSV” to download the summary.

## Staff page

- Filter staff by role and search by name or email.
- Click “Add personnel” to add a new doctor, assistant, administrator, or janitor. Fill in bio and salary details, then save.
- For each staff member, see role, contact data, base salary, last payment date, and total profit generated (for doctors).
